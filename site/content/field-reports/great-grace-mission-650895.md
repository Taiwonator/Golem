# GREAT GRACE MISSION EMPOWERMENT PROGRAM

###  ILARO, OGUN STATE ON SEPTEMBER 9 ,2023

Golem Nigeria Outreach in Junction with Great Grace Mission held an empowerment program for widows , orphans and less privileged in the city of Ilaro , Ogun State on September 9, 2023. 

It was an event that witnessed the presence of widows, orphans, and the less privileged from different parts of Yewa."

The event started as soon as the Golem NIgeria Outreach team arrived  at 11:50 am  with an opening prayer from the church  leader followed by praises and thanksgiving lead by the Great Grace Mission team.

Later , a short  history of Great Grace Mission was briefed by Pastor Asamu followed by a short address from the Director of Golem Nigeria , Deacon Tunde Bamidele.

The presentation of writing materials to school pupils was done afterwards and then the meal items were shared to the widows accordingly. 

The occasion came to an end with  a vote of thanks from on of the widows and then a closing prayer by one of the church pastors. 

The program came to a halt at 1:45 pm.
