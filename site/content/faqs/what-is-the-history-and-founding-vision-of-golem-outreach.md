---
question: 'What is the history and founding vision of Golem Outreach?'
---

Golem Outreach, also known as God of Love Emancipation Ministries, was founded in 1998 by Jonathan and Alice Taiwo. It started as an informal effort to support Christian ministries and evolved into a formal organization in 2012. The charity's vision focuses on propagating the Christian gospel globally and providing practical support to the needy, particularly in Nigeria. Jonathan Taiwo, a Development Economist and Registered Mental Health Practitioner, currently serves as the Director of Programmes, while Alice Taiwo, an Auditor and Financial Accountant, has been instrumental in the organization's financial management.
