## Pure Religion inc Partner with GOLEM for Widows Empowerment Scheme 

Earlier today the Pure Religion INC, an Abuja-based NGO, partnered with God Of Love Emancipation Ministries (GOLEM) to distribute gift items to over 34 widows from various parts of the town.

During the event, Pure  Religion INC representatives , Miss Areo highlighted the NGO's mission focused on assisting orphans and widows. Mrs. Ayeni, from GOLEM, elaborated on their mission centered around love and referenced the organization's various initiatives. She encouraged widows to extend acts of love in their communities.

Pure Religion INC, renowned for its empowerment programs, expressed dedication to understanding the specific needs of widows, with plans to provide items such as grinding machines and sewing machines.

The widows conveyed heartfelt gratitude for the generous gifts.
